Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp of burnt jeera
  • 4 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp of salt
  • 1 tbsp of latin soy sauce
  • 4 spoons of olive cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala

Instructions

1. Fry onions until golden brown.

2. Add garlic and chicken, cook until browned.

3. Add the burnt jeera,pilau masala,tomato paste and the soy sauce then stir well.

4. Wash the rice well and it in and stir.

5. Add water and some salt for flavour.

6. Wait for it to cook and once all the water has dried up serve while hot with some salad.

This dish is a good source of protein. Chicken helps in muscle growth and repair. It is good in energy boosting. Rice provides carbs for sustained energy. It is rich in antioxidants. Spices like garlic and jeera have anti-inflammatory properties.
