Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 1 fish
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • corriander leaves
  • 2 royco cubes and 1 green pepper

Instructions

1. Boil water and add maize flour while stirring till cooked.

2. On a pan fry the onions till golden brown.

3. Add tomatoes and stir till a thick paste is formed, add the green pepper.

4. Add some water and the royco cubes with some salt.

5. let the soup simmer and add the fish in till hardly soft.

6. Add the corriander leaves when almost ready and serve while hot.

This delicious meal is rich in proteins as fish provides essential amino acids for body repair and growth.
